# Topcatyo — Profile Summary

> Based on over 11,383 forum posts, 10 sampled threads, and a very strong Newgrounds creative presence.

Topcatyo was a prominent and highly prolific mid-to-late era Clock Crew member. Standing as one of the community's most competent animators and musicians, Topcatyo successfully bridged the gap between classic Clock Crew forum shitposting and actual Newgrounds portal success.

## Identity
- **Alias**: Topcatyo. Known casually as "Action" in some threads (e.g., "Topcatyo and Action were watching TV one day...").
- **Outside Links**: Maintained a Bandcamp and Soundcloud under the moniker "supaflyarturo," indicating a strong background in music production.
- **Vibe**: A sharp-tongued, highly active artist who didn't suffer fools, but was heavily respected for his actual creative output.

## Artistic Talent & Output
Unlike members whose fame rested solely on forum drama, Topcatyo achieved massive success on the main Newgrounds portal:
- **Newgrounds Fame**: Accrued 269 fans and curated 738 blams/657 saves. 
- **Filmography**: He produced extremely high-quality and varied content. His portfolio ranges from absurdly high-effort parody animations ("The SHOCKING TRUTH About Korok Seeds" (4.08), "Fairy Tales if the Kids Were Smart" (4.22)) to a multi-part animated original series "Dungeons and DrawBombs." He also produced a "Critter Cam" series and high-quality voice/music work.
- **Audio Works**: Actively promoted his audio submissions on the portal (*"None of my stuff is any good, since they were all made in less than a hour."* despite clear talent).

## Personality & Humor
Topcatyo has a razor-sharp, occasionally ruthless edge perfectly aligned with the Clock Crew's "tough love" ecosystem.
- **Brutally Honest**: Not afraid to completely dismiss poorly constructed posts: 
  > *"I barely understood your pointless thread post. If you were really in university, you would have shot yourself before posting this."*
- **Sarcasm as a Language**: Reacts to bad art and bad ideas with pure sarcasm (*"I kinda wanna die after seeing that,"* or *"I hope you are joking."*).
- **The "Fact" Meta**: Was heavily involved in the forum's ongoing "Fact:" thread meme, specifically using it to chain music lyrics (quoting everything from Jimi Hendrix's *Purple Haze* to Tears for Fears' *Mad World*).

## Community Role
- **The Competent Veteran**: Topcatyo served as an unofficial mentor figure by sheer virtue of his high-quality Flash output. He was heavily sought after for collabs ("Topcatyo D'AAAAAAAAAW :3 collab").
- **Gatekeeper**: Openly mocked newer members who put zero effort into their personas: 
  > *"Do yourself a favor and don't just make your clock your username and then pretend that's perfectly kosher."*
- **Music/Audio Guide**: As one of the few members with genuine audio expertise, he regularly helped other Clocks source or critique their flash soundtracks.

## Interests & Tastes
- **Cinema & Media Check-ins**: Frequently posted detailed movie reviews, breaking down films like *Time Bandits*, *Detroit Rock City*, and *Kiss Kiss Bang Bang* with numerical scores and plot synopses.
- **Musical Eclecticism**: Quoted a wide range of lyrics (Jimi Hendrix, Tears For Fears, Rage Against the Machine) and composed his own original music.

## Timeline
- **Rise to Prominence**: Found his footing in the late 2000s, actively calling out bad 2006/2007-era clock names ("Dispencer Clock").
- **Peak Output**: Consistently released high-scoring 4.0+ animations throughout the 2010s to recent history.
- **Return / Perseverance**: After disappearing temporarily, he famously declared his return: *"My computer blew up and I was down and out for a while but I got a new fast one and here I am so hi guys gosh how bout those Seahawks."*
