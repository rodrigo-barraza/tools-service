# PhantomCatClock — Profile Summary

> Based on a substantial 9,331 forum posts spanning from March 06, 2006 to August 25, 2012, alongside 10 sampled threads and strong Newgrounds metadata.

PhantomCatClock (often shortened to PhantomCat) was a prolific and long-running member of the Clock Crew forums. Known for his custom title "the most boring and generic c" and a tendency toward casual, rapid-fire observations, he operated squarely within the core social engine of the site over his 6 years of peak activity.

## Identity & Timeline
- **Aliases**: PhantomCatClock, PhantomCat
- **Years Active**: March 2006 through August 2012.
- **Custom Title**: "the most boring and generic c"

## Artistic Talent & Output
Unlike users who only participated in drama, PhantomCat laid down a heavy footprint on Newgrounds.
- **Newgrounds Curation**: Amassed an impressive 4,522 Saves and 704 Blams, indicating roughly 5,000 flashes personally curated and voted on to protect the NG Portal.
- **Filmography**: He released several highly-rated collaborations and solos, including "V is a Cunt" (4.15) and "PhantomCat on TV" (3.97).
- **Artistic Role**: In the forums, he was active in evaluating other people's designs and sharing works-in-progress: *"A clock that isn't really named. Needs a name really bad. So post it here. Don't let it bother you that it's made in like 14 frames."*

## Personality & Humor
- **Blunt but Constructive**: He gave feedback freely but bluntly. *"Looks pretty cool despite it basically being a circle shaped with clock legs attached to it."*
- **Social Instigator**: He enjoyed kicking off community-driven art or improv exercises. He frequently started prompt threads like *"You have 5 mins to come up with one line for a funny flash"* or *"Draw a zombie / clock hybrid"*, pulling the community into collective creative bursts.
- **Apathetic Sarcasm**: Embraced the standard forum edginess with a bored twist (e.g., *"Wait wait wait. So now someone tells me?"*).

## Community Role
- **Event Promotor**: He aggressively promoted new Clock Crew Flash releases on Newgrounds within the forum (*"New CC flash is up at NG"*). 
- **The Ground-Level Regular**: While not a super-moderator like Flounderman, PhantomCat was in the trenches of the art and spam forums, casually keeping threads alive and directing traffic to the portal. 

## Interests

- **Off-topic Discussions**: Started threads on everything from personal hardware setups to casual complaints (*"Holy shit it's hot", "New computer!", "I hate to be 'That guy', but..."*).
